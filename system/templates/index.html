Guinean Students Association at Borough of Manhattan Community College(GSABMCC)

The Guinean Students Association (GSA) is an organization that aims to assist and provide resources to students in our community and empowers them through education. This organization was founded by Abdourahamane Diallo, former GSA Baruch President, in spring 2016. Upon gathering a group of friends with a mutual desire to create a club that will give back to the community and help every student succeed academically and professionally.Then in 2018 this organization is implemented at Borough of Manhattan Community College (BMCC) by Boubacar Bah. The mission of GSABMCC is to handle the challenges that student faced and assist them throughout their academic year to build leaders that will change the future and contribute in social services throughout the community.
